About us

We are a newly opened Nepalese and Indian Restaurant in Southampton.

We are here to serve Nepalese and Indian traditional food with friendly manner of service together with great quality and freshly prepare food by Nepalese Chefs with their own style at its most authentic and vibrant form. Our aim is to stand out from the crowd to enhance Traditional Nepalese food in most significant way as in Nepal.
